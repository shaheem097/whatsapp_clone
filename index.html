WhatsApp Business App

WhatsApp Business is a free to download app that was built with
the small business owner in mind. Create a catalog to showcase
your products and services. Connect with your customers easily
by using tools to automate, sort and quickly respond to
messages.

WhatsApp can also help medium and large businesses provide
customer support and deliver important notifications to
customers. Learn more about Business API.

END-TO-END ENCRYPTION

Security by Default

Some of your most personal moments are shared on WhatsApp,
which is why we built end-to-end encryption into the latest
versions of our app. When end-to-end encrypted, your messages
and calls are secured so only you and the person you're
communicating with can read or listen to them, and nobody in
between, not even WhatsApp.
